Component Library for Study Space

Duration: September 2021

Engagement Type: Interaction Design Course (711)

Objective

This project was a part of the Interaction Design Studio course at UMD. The goal of the project was to come up with an idea of an application and design a component library for it.

Coming up with the application

One of my friends shared that she did not like the idea of studying at home. She frequently visits library to study. Over the past semester, she got bored of going to the same place everyday and wished if she knew more places to study. That‚Äôs how I got my idea for the application -

An app that helps student find places to study (indoor and outdoor) throughout the campus.

Exploring Android and iOS Design Systems

I explored the Android and iOS Design Systems before starting to design my own component library. I read the HIG and material guidelines to understand the rationale behind design choices for these systems. Finally, I designed my app using them.
